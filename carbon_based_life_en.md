# Carbon Based Life

*Little daily things for carbon-based life*

---

## What can you do today?

Max 3 things per day. Or don't.

---

### Morning

- Drink some water, feel the temperature
- Look outside, not at your phone
- Stretch
- Smile at yourself in the mirror
- Take a few deep breaths

---

### Afternoon

- Stand up and walk around
- Touch a plant nearby
- Stare into space
- Look at the sky
- Say something casual to someone

---

### Evening

- Turn off the lights, sit for a while
- Think about which moment today was nice
- Put your phone far away
- Sleep early
